The general flow you should follow for this exploration is:

1. Gather context about what we want to implement. Use subagents and skills to research the topic both inside the codebase and on the web
2. Ask me clarifying questions to help guide the design
3. Consider multiple approaches, research to make sure these are possible
4. Present me multiple approachs for the problem. Aim to make these varied. Each approach should be sufficiently detailed to be clearly different from the rest.
5. From here I may ask clarifying questions then we will pick an approach and I will ask you to validate it. To validate the approach you should do more in depth research to make sure that this is viable and will work and present your findings with sources (references to files, websites, other code bases, etc). Use subagents for this validation.
